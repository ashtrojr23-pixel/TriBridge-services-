What does LGBTQ+ stand for? ‚ñº
LGBTQ+ stands for Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual, Transgender, Queer (or Questioning), and + represents many other identities including Intersex, Asexual, Pansexual, Non-binary, and more.
What is gender identity vs sexual orientation? ‚ñº
Gender identity is your internal sense of your own gender ‚Äî man, woman, non-binary, or another identity. Sexual orientation is about who you are attracted to romantically or sexually.
What does it mean to be non-binary? ‚ñº
Non-binary is a term for gender identities that don't fit exclusively into "man" or "woman." Non-binary people may identify as both, neither, or somewhere between.
Is being LGBTQ+ a choice? ‚ñº
No. Sexual orientation and gender identity are not choices. Every major medical and psychological organisation affirms that being LGBTQ+ is a natural variation of human identity.
How can I be a better ally? ‚ñº
Listen without judgment, use correct names and pronouns, speak up against discrimination, educate yourself, and make your spaces actively welcoming.
Where can I find LGBTQ+ support in Kenya? ‚ñº
TriBridge Services offers confidential, LGBTQ+-affirming peer counselling. You can also reach GALCK+ (Gay and Lesbian Coalition of Kenya).
